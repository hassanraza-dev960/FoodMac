Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver from 10:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily. Online orders are accepted 24/7 and will be processed during operating hours.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We provide catering services for events, parties, and corporate functions. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, mobile banking, and popular digital wallets like JazzCash and EasyPaisa.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 25-35 minutes. During peak hours, it may take up to 45 minutes. We'll keep you updated with real-time tracking.
